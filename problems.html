Major Problems Facing Our Planet

Understanding the issues is the first step to solving them.

Pollution

Pollution of air, water, and land is severely impacting ecosystems and human health worldwide.

Climate Change

Rising temperatures, melting ice caps, and sea level rise threaten communities and biodiversity.

Deforestation

Forests are being destroyed for agriculture, timber, and development, endangering countless species.

Plastic Waste

Plastic takes hundreds of years to degrade and is polluting our oceans and harming marine life.

Water Scarcity

Over 2 billion people lack access to safe drinking water due to pollution and overuse.

Biodiversity Loss

Human activities are causing the extinction of species at an alarming rate, destabilizing ecosystems.
